How to Reach Our Clinic

We are located in one of Islamabad's most accessible commercial areas — three simple steps and you're at our door.

1

Come to Fazal-ul-Haq Road

Head to Fazal-ul-Haq Road in Blue Area, Islamabad's central commercial district — easily reachable from Jinnah Avenue and accessible by car, taxi, or ride-hailing services.

2

Look for Tehzeeb Bakers

Our building — Muhammad Gulistan Khan House, 89 North — is directly adjacent to Tehzeeb Bakers, one of the area's best-known landmarks. You can't miss it.

3

Visit Office B-9, Basement

Take the stairs to the basement level and find Office B-9 — our clinic. Walk in during opening hours, or message us on WhatsApp and we'll be ready to receive you.
