Hearing Aid in I-Sectors Islamabad - I-8, I-9, I-10, I-14

March 30, 2026 Location Guide 5 min read

The I-sectors of Islamabad are among the city's most rapidly developing residential zones. From the established neighborhoods of I-8 near the Faizabad interchange to the expanding communities of I-14 along the Islamabad Expressway, thousands of families call these sectors home. If you or a family member needs a hearing aid, our authorized Signia center in Blue Area is your most reliable option, and we also offer delivery for those who cannot visit in person.

Reaching Our Blue Area Center from I-Sectors

The I-sectors connect to Blue Area through several well-known routes. Here is what to expect:

  • I-8 (near Faizabad): 10-15 minutes via Murree Road and then Jinnah Avenue. The Faizabad interchange is a major junction, and from there Blue Area is a straight drive west along Jinnah Avenue. During peak Faizabad traffic, use the alternative route through G-9.
  • I-9 (Industrial Area): 12-15 minutes. Head towards the IJP Road, then connect to Khayaban-e-Suhrwardy leading to Blue Area. Alternatively, take the 9th Avenue connector towards the Secretariat.
  • I-10 (I-10 Markaz): 15-18 minutes via the IJP Road or through I-9 connecting to 9th Avenue. The route is mostly signal-free once you reach the main arteries.
  • I-14 (near Expressway): 20-25 minutes. Take the Islamabad Expressway north, exit at Faizabad, and follow the Jinnah Avenue route to Blue Area. Alternatively, use the Kashmir Highway exit for a less congested path.

Special Considerations for I-9 Industrial Area Workers

Workers and business owners in the I-9 Industrial Area are often exposed to high noise levels from machinery, vehicles, and manufacturing processes. This prolonged noise exposure can accelerate hearing loss. If you work in I-9 and have noticed difficulty following conversations, ringing in your ears (tinnitus), or the need to increase TV volume, it is time for a professional hearing test.

We recommend the Signia Motion series for industrial workers because of its superior noise reduction technology. These hearing aids can distinguish between speech and background noise, amplifying voices while reducing the roar of machinery. The tinnitus therapy feature available in the Motion 1Nx and above is also helpful for those experiencing ringing after years of noise exposure.
