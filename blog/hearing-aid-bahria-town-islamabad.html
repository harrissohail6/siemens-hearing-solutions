Why Buy from an Authorized Dealer Instead of Local Shops

Bahria Town has pharmacies and general medical shops that occasionally sell hearing amplifiers. These are not the same as professional hearing aids. Here is the difference:

  • Amplifiers vs hearing aids: An amplifier makes everything louder equally. A hearing aid selectively amplifies the frequencies you have lost, based on your audiogram. This protects your remaining hearing.
  • Programming: Every Signia hearing aid we sell is programmed using Connexx software, matched precisely to your hearing test results. Generic amplifiers cannot be programmed.
  • Warranty: All our Signia hearing aids come with a manufacturer warranty. Amplifiers from pharmacies have no warranty or after-sales support.
  • Longevity: A properly maintained Signia hearing aid lasts 5-7 years. A cheap amplifier typically fails within 6-12 months.

Hearing Tests for Bahria Town Residents

If you do not have a recent audiogram, you have two options. You can visit any ENT specialist or audiologist within Bahria Town or at nearby hospitals like Shifa International or Ali Medical Centre to get a PTA hearing test. Alternatively, drive to our Blue Area center where we provide professional hearing tests on-site. The test is complimentary with any hearing aid purchase.

We recommend getting your hearing tested if you are over 55, have been exposed to loud environments, or notice any of these signs: asking people to repeat themselves, increasing the TV volume beyond what others find comfortable, or difficulty following conversations in groups.
