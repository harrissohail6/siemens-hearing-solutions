Hearing Aids for Weddings & Events - Don't Miss a Word

January 24, 2026 Tips & Care 5 min read

Pakistani weddings are joyous celebrations filled with music, laughter, conversations, and excitement. But for hearing aid users, the extremely loud music, crowded marquees, and multiple simultaneous conversations can make these events overwhelming. With the right preparation and hearing aid settings, you can enjoy every moment of these special occasions.

Did You Know: Pakistani wedding halls and marquees can reach noise levels of 90-110 decibels during musical performances, which is equivalent to a rock concert. This is not just challenging for hearing aid users but can be harmful to everyone's hearing. Proper hearing aid settings are essential in these environments.

Preparing Your Hearing Aids Before the Event

  • Charge Fully or Use Fresh Batteries: Events can last 4-6 hours or more. Ensure your rechargeable hearing aids are fully charged or that you have spare batteries for battery-powered models.
  • Create an Event Program: Ask your audiologist to create a dedicated program for loud events that reduces overall gain while maintaining speech clarity. This can be saved as a separate program in your hearing aids.
  • Clean Your Hearing Aids: A clean hearing aid performs better. Clean the microphones, sound outlets, and replace the wax guard if needed.
  • Download the App: If your Signia hearing aids support app control, make sure the Signia app is installed so you can adjust settings in real time during the event.

During the Wedding or Event

Managing Loud Music

During the music and dancing portions, your hearing aids may amplify the already-loud music to uncomfortable levels. Use the Signia app or the volume button to reduce amplification. If you have a dedicated loud event program, switch to it. Some users prefer to remove their hearing aids during the loudest musical performances and put them back on for conversation time.

Conversation Strategies

For important conversations during the event, move to quieter areas away from speakers and the dance floor. The lobby, garden area, or corner seating typically have significantly lower noise levels. Face the person you are speaking with and ask them to speak clearly rather than shouting, which actually distorts speech.

Protecting Your Hearing

Even with hearing loss, extremely loud music can cause further damage. If the music is painfully loud with your hearing aids in, it is better to remove them temporarily. Your hearing aids have output limiting features, but prolonged exposure to very high volumes is not advisable.

Best Hearing Aid Styles for Events

  • Discreet Options: If you prefer not to show your hearing aids at a formal event, CIC hearing aids sit completely inside the ear canal and are virtually invisible.
  • Best Performance: RIC hearing aids with advanced directional microphones offer the best noise management for loud environments.
  • Reliable Choice: BTE hearing aids are durable and handle varying sound levels well, making them dependable for long events.

After the Event

After attending a loud event, give your ears a rest. Remove your hearing aids and spend some time in a quiet environment. Clean your hearing aids as sweat and environmental particles from the event may have accumulated. If you notice any changes in your hearing after an extremely loud event, schedule a hearing test to check for any changes.

With the right preparation and settings, hearing aid users can fully participate in and enjoy Pakistani weddings, milads, dawats, and all other celebrations. Do not let hearing loss keep you from the moments that matter most.
