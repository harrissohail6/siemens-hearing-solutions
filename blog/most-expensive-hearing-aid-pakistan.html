Top Premium Hearing Aids in Pakistan

Signia Pure Charge & Go AX - From Rs. 585,000/pair

The Signia Pure Charge & Go AX represents the pinnacle of hearing aid technology. It features Augmented Xperience (AX) technology that processes speech and background sounds separately for unprecedented clarity. Key features include:

  • 48 channels of sound processing
  • Rechargeable lithium-ion battery with all-day power
  • Bluetooth streaming for phone calls, music, and TV
  • Signia App for smartphone-based adjustments
  • Own Voice Processing (OVP) for natural self-voice
  • Tinnitus therapy signals built-in

Signia Motion Charge & Go - Premium BTE

For those who need more power, the Signia Motion series offers premium BTE hearing aids with rechargeable batteries and Bluetooth connectivity. With up to 48 channels, these devices provide excellent performance for severe to profound hearing loss.

Signia Silk X - Premium Invisible

The Signia Silk is a premium invisible hearing aid that sits deep in the ear canal. Unlike custom CIC models, Silk comes ready to wear with soft silicone click sleeves. It offers premium sound processing in the smallest possible form factor.

What Makes Premium Hearing Aids Worth It

Speech in Noise Performance

Premium hearing aids use advanced algorithms to isolate speech from background noise. In a busy restaurant, mosque, or family gathering, a premium device helps you follow conversations that a basic hearing aid would struggle with.

Bluetooth Connectivity

Stream phone calls, music, YouTube, and TV audio directly to your hearing aids. This feature alone transforms the hearing experience for many users who previously struggled with phone conversations.

Rechargeable Convenience

No more buying and replacing tiny batteries. Place your hearing aids in the charging case at night and enjoy a full day of power, including hours of Bluetooth streaming.

Smartphone App Control

Adjust volume, change programs, and fine-tune your hearing aids from your smartphone. The Signia app also offers remote support, allowing our audiologist to make adjustments without you visiting the clinic.
