Hearing Loss in Gujranwala's Industrial Community

Gujranwala's economy is built on manufacturing. The city produces a large percentage of Pakistan's electric fans, rice processing equipment, and metal goods. Workers in these industries face daily exposure to machinery noise that often exceeds safe levels. Combined with traffic noise on busy GT Road and the general hustle of the city, hearing loss is a growing concern.

Elderly residents in Gujranwala also face age-related hearing loss (presbycusis), which gradually reduces the ability to hear high-frequency sounds like children's voices and doorbells. Modern hearing aids can address both noise-induced and age-related hearing loss effectively.

Signia Hearing Aid Models for Gujranwala
