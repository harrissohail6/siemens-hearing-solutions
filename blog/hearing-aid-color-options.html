Hearing Aid Color Options - Match Your Skin & Hair Tone

February 10, 2026 Tips & Care 5 min read

Modern hearing aids come in a variety of colors designed to blend with your skin tone, match your hair color, or even make a style statement. Gone are the days when hearing aids only came in beige. Today's Signia hearing aids offer multiple color options that help you wear your device with confidence.

Color Choices: Signia hearing aids are available in multiple colors including skin tone shades, hair-matching colors, and modern metallic finishes. Ask our audiologist about available colors for your chosen model. Email contact@siemenshearingsolutions.com.

Color Categories for Hearing Aids

Skin Tone Colors - For Maximum Discretion

If you want your hearing aid to blend in and be as invisible as possible, choose a color that matches your skin tone. Signia offers several skin tone shades:

  • Beige: A classic light skin tone that works well for fair to medium complexions common in northern Pakistan.
  • Medium Brown: Ideal for the wheat-to-medium complexion prevalent across Pakistan.
  • Dark Brown: Suitable for darker skin tones.

Hair-Matching Colors - Behind-the-Ear Blending

For BTE and RIC hearing aids that sit behind the ear, matching your hair color can be more effective than matching skin tone:

  • Black: The most popular choice in Pakistan, blending perfectly with dark hair.
  • Dark Grey: Suits grey or salt-and-pepper hair, common among older users.
  • Silver: A modern, metallic option for grey-haired users.

Modern and Bold Colors

Some users, particularly younger ones, prefer to treat their hearing aid as an accessory rather than hiding it:

  • Rose Gold: A trendy option popular among women.
  • Titanium: A sleek, modern metallic finish.
  • White: Clean and modern, works well with light-colored hair or as a style choice.

Color Tips for Pakistani Users

Based on our experience serving customers across Pakistan, here are our top color recommendations:

  • For Men: Black or dark brown are the most popular choices. Black blends with hair, while dark brown works well for skin tone matching.
  • For Women Who Wear Hijab: Since the hearing aid may be covered by the hijab, any color works. However, beige or skin-tone colors are preferred for moments when the scarf is adjusted.
  • For Women Without Hijab: Black for dark hair, or medium brown for skin tone matching.
  • For Children: Bright colors or fun patterns can help children feel positive about wearing their hearing aid.
  • For CIC Users: CIC hearing aids sit inside the ear canal, so color is less important. They typically come in skin-tone or clear/transparent options.
Color Availability: Not all colors are available in all models. Some entry-level models come in limited colors (usually beige and black), while premium models offer a wider palette. Ask us about specific color availability for your chosen model. Email contact@siemenshearingsolutions.com.

Does Color Affect Performance?

No. The color of your hearing aid has absolutely no effect on its performance or sound quality. It is purely a cosmetic choice. Choose whatever color makes you most comfortable and confident wearing your hearing aid. View all our models on the products page.
