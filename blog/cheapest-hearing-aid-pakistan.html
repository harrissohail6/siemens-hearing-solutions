Cheap vs. Genuine: Know the Difference

You may find hearing devices on online marketplaces for Rs. 2,000-10,000. These are NOT hearing aids. They are personal sound amplifiers that:

  • Amplify all sounds equally, including background noise
  • Cannot be programmed for your specific hearing loss
  • May cause further hearing damage with improper amplification
  • Have no warranty or after-sales support
  • Break within weeks or months

A genuine hearing aid like the Signia Prompt SP at Rs. 35,000 is digitally programmed to amplify only the frequencies you need, based on your audiogram. It protects your remaining hearing while improving your ability to hear speech and important sounds.
