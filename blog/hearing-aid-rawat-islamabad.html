Available Hearing Aid Models

We bring the complete Signia lineup to Rawat residents at transparent, fixed prices with no hidden charges:

BTE (Behind-the-Ear) Hearing Aids

Perfect for moderate to severe hearing loss, our BTE hearing aids are the most popular choice. The Signia Prompt SP starts at just Rs. 35,000, offering 4-channel digital processing in a reliable package. The premium Signia Motion series with Bluetooth and up to 48 channels is available for those wanting the best technology.

CIC (Completely-in-Canal) Hearing Aids

Invisible when worn, CIC hearing aids are custom-molded to your ear canal. Starting from Rs. 35,000, they are ideal for those who want discretion without compromising on sound quality.

RIC (Receiver-in-Canal) Hearing Aids

Our RIC models offer the best balance of performance and aesthetics. Lightweight, comfortable, and nearly invisible, they start from Rs. 85,000 and are perfect for mild to moderately severe hearing loss.

Rechargeable Hearing Aids

The Signia Pure Charge and Go series eliminates battery hassles with overnight charging for all-day use. Available from Rs. 585,000 per pair, these premium devices include Bluetooth streaming for phone calls and music.
