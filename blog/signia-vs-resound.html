Signia vs ReSound Hearing Aids - Detailed Comparison

February 24, 2026 Buying Guide 6 min read

Signia and ReSound are both globally recognized hearing aid brands that offer advanced technology. Signia, backed by the Siemens legacy from Germany, and ReSound, a Danish brand under the GN Group, each bring unique strengths to the table. This comparison will help Pakistani buyers understand the differences and choose wisely.

Quick Comparison: Signia offers better availability and more affordable entry points in Pakistan (from Rs. 35,000). ReSound pioneered Made-for-iPhone hearing aids and excels in wireless connectivity but has limited dealer presence in Pakistan.

Brand Overview

Signia inherits Siemens' 140+ years of engineering excellence. The brand is part of WS Audiology, the world's largest hearing aid group. ReSound is part of GN Group, a Danish company founded in 1869. ReSound was the first brand to introduce Made-for-iPhone (MFi) hearing aids in 2014, revolutionizing hearing aid connectivity.

Sound Technology

Signia's Approach

Signia uses Own Voice Processing (OVP) to ensure your own voice sounds natural. Their Augmented Xperience (AX) platform splits sound into two separate processors - one for your own voice and one for surrounding sounds - delivering unmatched clarity in conversations.

ReSound's Approach

ReSound uses Organic Hearing technology designed to mimic how the brain naturally processes sound. Their M&RIE (Microphone & Receiver-in-Ear) design places a microphone inside the ear canal, utilizing the natural shape of your ear to deliver more natural directional hearing.

Connectivity Features

ReSound has historically led in connectivity, being the first to offer direct iPhone streaming. Today, both brands offer comprehensive connectivity:

  • Signia: Bluetooth streaming to Android and iPhone. Signia App for remote adjustments. TeleCare for remote audiologist sessions.
  • ReSound: Bluetooth LE Audio, direct streaming to iPhone and select Android phones. ReSound Smart 3D app. Cloud-based remote fine-tuning.

Price and Availability in Pakistan

This is the decisive factor for most Pakistani buyers:

  • Signia: Widely available through authorized dealers. BTE from Rs. 35,000, CIC from Rs. 35,000, RIC from Rs. 85,000. View full pricing.
  • ReSound: Limited authorized dealers in Pakistan. Higher entry prices. Service and warranty support harder to find outside major cities.
Bottom Line: Both brands make excellent hearing aids. For Pakistani buyers, Signia wins on availability, affordability, after-sales support, and brand recognition (Siemens). ReSound is a great choice if you prioritize cutting-edge connectivity and can find reliable local support.

Which Is Right for You?

If you value reliable after-sales service, a wide range of price options, and the trusted Siemens name, Signia is the clear choice. If you are an iPhone user who prioritizes wireless streaming quality and can access a ReSound dealer, that brand also has merit. For most Pakistani hearing aid buyers, the practical advantages of Signia make it the recommended choice.
