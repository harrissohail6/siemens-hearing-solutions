Residents of Islamabad's F-sectors enjoy some of the shortest commutes to our authorized Signia hearing aid center in Blue Area. Whether you live near F-6 Supermarket, shop at F-7 Markaz, or reside in the quieter streets of F-10 and F-11, our clinic is just a few minutes away by car. This page covers everything F-sector residents need to know about getting hearing aids close to home.

How Close Are the F-Sectors to Our Center?

Blue Area is essentially the commercial extension of the F-sector grid. Here are approximate driving times from each major F-sector to our clinic:

  • F-6 (Supermarket area): 5-7 minutes via Nazimuddin Road. Head towards Jinnah Avenue and you are practically at our doorstep.
  • F-7 (Jinnah Supermarket): 7-8 minutes via Faisal Avenue. Take the Margalla Road exit and merge onto Jinnah Avenue heading east.
  • F-8 (F-8 Markaz): 8-10 minutes via Faisal Avenue or the Nazimuddin Road route. Both lead directly to Blue Area without any major traffic bottlenecks.
  • F-10 (F-10 Markaz): 10-12 minutes. Take Faisal Avenue towards Faisal Mosque and continue straight to Blue Area. The route is well-maintained and traffic-light controlled.
  • F-11 (F-11 Markaz): 12-15 minutes via the Margalla Road or through F-10 connecting to Faisal Avenue. Even during peak hours, the drive rarely exceeds 20 minutes.
Tip for F-Sector Residents: If you are coming from F-6 or F-7, you can even walk to our Blue Area center in about 15-20 minutes. The route along Jinnah Avenue has wide, shaded pavements suitable for a comfortable walk.
