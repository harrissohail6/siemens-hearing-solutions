What to Expect During Your Visit

When you visit our clinic for the first time, here is what the experience looks like:

  • Reception and Consultation: Our team will welcome you and discuss your hearing concerns. We ask about your symptoms, medical history, and lifestyle to understand your needs.
  • Hearing Test: If you do not already have a recent audiogram, we will conduct a professional PTA (Pure Tone Audiometry) test. This painless test takes about 20-30 minutes and identifies the exact type and degree of your hearing loss.
  • Product Recommendation: Based on your test results, budget, and preferences, our audiologist will recommend suitable hearing aid models from our Signia product range.
  • Trial and Fitting: You can try the recommended models in the clinic to experience the sound quality firsthand. Once you choose, the hearing aid is professionally programmed to your audiogram using Signia's Connexx software.
  • Take Home Same Day: Many popular models are available for immediate fitting, so you can walk out with your new hearing aid the same day you visit.
What to Bring: If you have a recent hearing test report (audiogram), bring it along. If you are currently using a hearing aid, bring that too so our audiologist can assess it. Bring a family member if possible, as they can help during the consultation and provide feedback on your hearing improvement.
