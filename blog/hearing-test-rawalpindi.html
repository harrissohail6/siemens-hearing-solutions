What to Expect at Your Hearing Test

Your visit will follow a systematic process designed to give us a complete picture of your hearing health:

  1. Registration and History: We take down your details and discuss your hearing concerns, medical history, and any medications you are taking.
  2. Otoscopy: A quick visual check of your ear canal and eardrum to rule out wax blockage or visible abnormalities.
  3. Tympanometry: A painless test that measures middle ear pressure and eardrum movement. Takes 2-3 minutes per ear.
  4. Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA): The main hearing test where you listen to tones through headphones and indicate when you hear them. Takes 15-20 minutes.
  5. Results Discussion: Your audiologist explains the audiogram and what it means for your hearing. If hearing aids are recommended, we discuss suitable options from our Signia product range.

The entire appointment takes approximately 30-45 minutes. You will leave with a printed audiogram and clear recommendations. For more details about our hearing tests, visit our Hearing Tests page.
