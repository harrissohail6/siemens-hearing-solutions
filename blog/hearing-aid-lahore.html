Available Hearing Aid Types for Lahore Customers

The most popular choice for Lahore customers, BTE hearing aids are reliable, powerful, and easy to use. Starting from Rs. 35,000, they are suitable for mild to severe hearing loss. Models include the Signia Prompt SP, Fun SP, and Motion series.

For customers in Lahore who prefer a discreet option, CIC hearing aids are nearly invisible when worn. Custom-made to fit inside your ear canal, these start from Rs. 35,000 and are ideal for mild to moderate hearing loss.
