Remote Hearing Aid Fitting - Adjust from Home via App

December 26, 2025Technology5 min read

What if you could have your hearing aids adjusted by your audiologist without leaving your home? With Signia's TeleCare remote fitting technology, this is now a reality. Whether you live in a remote area of Pakistan, have mobility challenges, or simply prefer the convenience of home service, remote hearing aid fitting brings professional audiological care directly to you through your smartphone.

How It Works: Your audiologist makes adjustments to your hearing aid settings remotely using Signia's TeleCare platform. The new settings are sent to your Signia app, which transfers them to your hearing aids via Bluetooth. No clinic visit required for routine adjustments.

What Is Remote Fitting?

Remote fitting (also called teleaudiology or TeleCare) allows your audiologist to modify your hearing aid programming from their computer while you are at home. Using the Signia app on your smartphone as a bridge, your audiologist can adjust volume levels, modify frequency responses, change program settings, and fine-tune noise reduction parameters.

Who Benefits Most

  • Patients in remote areas: People living in cities without hearing aid clinics, or in rural Pakistan, can receive professional adjustments without traveling to Islamabad
  • Elderly patients: Those with mobility issues who find clinic visits physically challenging
  • Busy professionals: People who cannot take time off work for a clinic appointment
  • Follow-up adjustments: Minor tweaks that do not require a full in-person evaluation
  • Overseas Pakistanis: People who purchased hearing aids during a visit to Pakistan and need adjustments after returning abroad

The Remote Fitting Process

Step 1: Report Your Experience

Through the Signia app, you can rate your hearing experience in different situations and send detailed feedback to your audiologist. Describe specific scenarios where you are struggling, such as difficulty hearing in restaurants or finding certain sounds too loud.

Step 2: Audiologist Reviews

Your audiologist reviews your feedback and usage data from the app. Modern hearing aids track how long you wear them, which programs you use most, and your listening environment statistics.

Step 3: Remote Adjustment

Based on your feedback, the audiologist creates new settings and sends them to your app. You receive a notification when new settings are ready to apply.

Step 4: Apply and Test

Open the Signia app and accept the new settings. Your hearing aids are immediately updated via Bluetooth. Try the new settings in your daily life and provide feedback for further refinement if needed.

For Patients Across Pakistan: We serve patients from Karachi, Lahore, Peshawar, Quetta, and everywhere in between. If you purchased your Signia hearing aids from us and need adjustments, our remote fitting service means you do not need to travel to Islamabad for every follow-up.

What Can Be Adjusted Remotely

  • Overall volume and gain levels
  • Frequency-specific amplification (boosting or reducing specific pitches)
  • Noise reduction strength
  • Speech enhancement settings
  • Program switching options
  • Wind noise management
  • Feedback cancellation sensitivity
  • Tinnitus masker settings

What Still Requires a Clinic Visit

While remote fitting covers most adjustment needs, some services still require an in-person visit:

  • Initial fitting: The first hearing aid fitting should always be done in person with a proper hearing test
  • Physical fit issues: If the ear mold or dome is uncomfortable, it needs physical modification
  • Real ear measurement: Verification of hearing aid output in your ear requires specialized equipment
  • Cleaning and repairs: Physical maintenance of the device
  • New audiograms: If your hearing has changed, a new hearing test is needed

Getting Started with Remote Fitting

To use remote fitting, you need a compatible Bluetooth-enabled Signia hearing aid and the Signia app installed on your smartphone. During your initial in-person fitting, our audiologist will set up TeleCare on your app and show you how to use it. After that, requesting a remote adjustment is as simple as sending us a WhatsApp message.
