How Our WhatsApp Ordering Works

We have streamlined the ordering process to be as simple as possible. Here is exactly how it works from start to finish:

Step 1: Initial Contact

Send us a message on WhatsApp at 0333-1911824. Tell us about your hearing concerns, and we will ask a few questions about your hearing loss history, any existing hearing aids, and your budget range.

Step 2: Share Your Audiogram

Take a photo of your most recent hearing test report (audiogram) and send it to us on WhatsApp. If you do not have one, we will recommend an ENT specialist or audiology center near your city where you can get a PTA hearing test. Most cities in Pakistan have hospitals or clinics offering this basic test.

Step 3: Receive Recommendations

Our audiologist reviews your audiogram and recommends 2-3 hearing aid models that match your hearing loss profile, lifestyle needs, and budget. We explain the features, pros, and cons of each option so you can make an informed decision.

Step 4: Confirm Your Order

Once you select a model, we confirm the order details including the hearing aid model, ear side (left, right, or both), payment amount, and delivery address. Payment can be made via bank transfer, Easypaisa, or JazzCash.

Step 5: Programming and Dispatch

We program the hearing aid to your audiogram using Signia's Connexx software, package it securely, and dispatch it through a trusted courier service. You receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment.

Step 6: Delivery and Remote Support

Once you receive your hearing aid, our audiologist schedules a WhatsApp video call to guide you through proper insertion, volume adjustment, and care instructions. We remain available for ongoing support.

Secure Packaging: Every hearing aid is packed in its original Signia box with all accessories, wrapped in bubble wrap, and placed inside a padded courier envelope. We use tamper-evident seals so you can verify the package has not been opened during transit. Insurance is included for all shipments.
