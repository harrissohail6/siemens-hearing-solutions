Noise-Induced Hearing Loss in Sialkot

Sialkot's economy thrives on manufacturing. From sports goods factories to surgical instrument workshops, the city's industrial zones are filled with machinery noise. Workers in leather tanning, football stitching machines, and metal forging operations face daily exposure to harmful noise levels that can exceed 90 decibels.

Over years of exposure, this leads to permanent sensorineural hearing loss. Early signs include difficulty understanding speech in noisy environments, asking people to repeat themselves, and turning up TV volume higher than others find comfortable. If you notice these symptoms, a hearing test and possibly a hearing aid can significantly improve your quality of life.
