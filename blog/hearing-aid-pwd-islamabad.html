Why Not Buy from Local Shops?

PWD's commercial market has general medical stores that may sell hearing amplifiers for a few thousand rupees. These devices look like hearing aids but function very differently. An amplifier simply makes all sounds louder, including background noise, traffic, and fans. A properly programmed Signia hearing aid selectively amplifies only the frequencies where your hearing has declined, giving you clear speech without uncomfortable noise.

Additionally, amplifiers from general stores come with no warranty, no programming, and no professional guidance. They often cause more frustration than benefit, leading people to believe that hearing aids do not work. If you have tried a cheap amplifier and been disappointed, we encourage you to try a properly fitted Signia hearing aid. The difference is dramatic.
