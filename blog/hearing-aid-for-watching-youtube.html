Stream YouTube & Netflix to Your Hearing Aids - How To

March 7, 2026Technology5 min read

Watching YouTube and Netflix is one of the most popular leisure activities in Pakistan. But for people with hearing loss, keeping up with dialogue is frustrating - especially when family complains about the TV volume. Bluetooth hearing aids offer an elegant solution: stream audio directly from your device to your ears at your preferred volume.

Game Changer: With Bluetooth Signia hearing aids, YouTube and Netflix audio streams directly into your ears at your preferred volume while everyone else hears the TV at their comfortable level. No more volume wars with family.

How Streaming Works

When you pair your Bluetooth Signia hearing aids with your smartphone or tablet, audio from any app streams directly to your hearing aids. The hearing aids function as personalized wireless headphones custom-tuned to your hearing profile. The audio is not just louder - it is optimized for your specific hearing loss pattern.

Streaming from Phones and Tablets

iPhone/iPad: After pairing via Settings > Accessibility > Hearing Devices, all audio from YouTube, Netflix, and any app automatically streams to your hearing aids.

Android: With ASHA-compatible devices (Samsung Galaxy S10+, Google Pixel 3+ and newer), all app audio streams to paired hearing aids.

Streaming from TV

Option 1: StreamLine TV Accessory

Signia offers the StreamLine TV transmitter that connects to your TV's audio output and wirelessly sends audio to your hearing aids with low latency.

Option 2: Via Your Phone

Watch content on your phone or tablet and audio streams directly through Bluetooth. The simplest approach requiring no extra accessories.

Family Benefit: Stream TV audio to your hearing aids while the TV speakers play at a comfortable volume for everyone else. Your family will appreciate not having the TV at maximum volume.

Tips for Better Streaming

  • Enable subtitles: Use as a backup. YouTube has auto-captions, Netflix offers Urdu subtitles.
  • Use the Signia app: Adjust treble and bass to optimize dialogue clarity while streaming.
  • Charge before binge watching: Streaming uses more battery. Ensure full charge for long sessions.

Compatible Streaming Services

  • YouTube: Free content, Urdu/Hindi videos - streams perfectly
  • Netflix: Dramas, movies with excellent audio quality
  • Amazon Prime Video: Growing Pakistani content
  • Disney+/Hotstar: Movies and series
  • ARY ZAP, Geo TV: Pakistani drama streaming works great
  • Quran apps: Stream recitation privately

Which Models Support Streaming?

The Signia Pure Charge&Go series (from Rs. 585,000/pair) offers the best streaming with built-in Bluetooth. For budget-conscious users, the StreamLine Mic accessory adds streaming to non-Bluetooth Signia hearing aids.
