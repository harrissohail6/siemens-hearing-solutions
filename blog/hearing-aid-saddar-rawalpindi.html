Why Choose Us Over Saddar Market Shops

Saddar bazaar has medical supply shops that sell hearing devices for Rs. 3,000 to Rs. 15,000. These are almost always Chinese-made amplifiers, not hearing aids. Here is why that matters:

  • No hearing test: Saddar shops sell devices without testing your hearing. They cannot know which frequencies you have lost or how much amplification you need. Our clinic performs a proper PTA hearing test before recommending any device.
  • No programming: A shop amplifier has one setting - loud. A Signia hearing aid is programmed with multiple channels, each adjusted to your specific hearing loss pattern. This gives you clear speech without making background noise unbearable.
  • No warranty: If a Saddar shop device breaks in a month, you have no recourse. Our Signia hearing aids come with a manufacturer warranty and full after-sales support.
  • Risk of further damage: An improperly amplified device can actually worsen your hearing by over-amplifying certain frequencies. A professionally fitted hearing aid protects your remaining hearing.
