What Makes the Best Hearing Center

Not all hearing aid shops are equal. The best hearing centers distinguish themselves through several key factors that directly impact the quality of care you receive:

Qualified Audiologists

The foundation of any good hearing center is its professional staff. Our clinic is staffed by certified audiologists who have completed formal education in audiology and have years of clinical experience. They understand the complexities of different types of hearing loss and can make accurate diagnoses and recommendations. An audiologist is not the same as a hearing aid salesperson, and this distinction matters enormously for your outcomes.

Professional Diagnostic Equipment

Accurate diagnosis requires proper equipment. Our clinic features a calibrated audiometer for Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA) testing, a tympanometer for middle ear function assessment, and Signia's Connexx fitting system for precise hearing aid programming. These are professional-grade instruments that are regularly calibrated to ensure accurate results.

Comprehensive Product Range

The best center offers solutions for every type of hearing loss and every budget. We stock the complete Signia hearing aid range including BTE, RIC, CIC, Click CIC, and rechargeable models. This means we can always match the right device to your specific needs rather than pushing a limited selection.

Sound-Treated Testing Environment

Hearing tests must be conducted in a controlled acoustic environment to produce reliable results. Our testing room is designed to minimize external noise interference, ensuring that your audiogram accurately reflects your hearing ability. Results from noisy environments can lead to incorrect hearing aid programming.

First Visit Experience: When you visit our clinic for the first time, expect a thorough 45-60 minute experience that includes a detailed case history, professional hearing assessment, results explanation in simple language, product recommendations tailored to your needs, and the opportunity to try hearing aids on the spot.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of hearing healthcare services under one roof:

  • Hearing Tests (PTA): Pure Tone Audiometry to determine the type, degree, and configuration of your hearing loss. Results available immediately.
  • Tympanometry: Middle ear pressure testing to identify conditions like fluid buildup, eardrum perforation, or ossicular chain problems.
  • Hearing Aid Fitting: Professional programming using Signia Connexx software, customized to your unique audiogram for optimal sound quality.
  • Real-Ear Measurement: Verification that the hearing aid output matches the prescribed amplification targets at your eardrum.
  • Custom Ear Molds: Precision ear impressions for BTE and custom hearing aids to ensure perfect fit and comfort.
  • Follow-Up Care: Free adjustment visits to fine-tune your hearing aid as you adapt to amplified sound.
  • Repair and Maintenance: Cleaning, troubleshooting, and repair services for all Signia models.
  • Home Delivery: Nationwide delivery service for customers who cannot visit the clinic.
