Price Comparison by Type

Budget Category (Rs. 35,000 - Rs. 75,000)

Entry-level BTE and CIC hearing aids with 4-16 channels. Suitable for quiet environments and basic daily use. Our Signia Prompt SP at Rs. 35,000 leads this category with reliable performance at the lowest price point.

Mid-Range Category (Rs. 75,000 - Rs. 200,000)

Advanced RIC and Click CIC hearing aids with 16-32 channels. Better noise management, directional microphones, and improved speech clarity. Good for social situations and moderately noisy environments.

Premium Category (Rs. 200,000 - Rs. 650,000+)

Top-tier hearing aids with 32-48 channels, Bluetooth, rechargeable batteries, smartphone app control, and advanced noise processing. Best for professionals, social individuals, and those who demand the highest performance.

What to Consider Beyond Price

  • Authorized Dealer: Always buy from an authorized dealer to ensure genuine products and valid warranty.
  • After-Sales Service: Check if the dealer offers programming, adjustments, and repairs.
  • Warranty: Genuine hearing aids come with manufacturer warranty. Verify this before buying.
  • Professional Fitting: A hearing aid needs to be programmed by an audiologist using your audiogram. Without proper fitting, even an expensive hearing aid will not work well.
