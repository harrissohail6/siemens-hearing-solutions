Hearing Aid Battery Price in Pakistan 2026 - All Sizes

December 30, 2025 Buying Guide 5 min read

Hearing aid batteries are an ongoing cost that every non-rechargeable hearing aid user needs to budget for. In Pakistan, battery prices vary significantly depending on brand, size, and where you buy them. This comprehensive guide covers current prices, how to choose the right battery, and tips to make your batteries last longer.

Battery Sizes at a Glance: All hearing aid batteries are zinc-air type and come in four standardized sizes identified by color: Size 10 (Yellow), Size 312 (Brown), Size 13 (Orange), and Size 675 (Blue). Your hearing aid uses only one specific size.

Current Battery Prices in Pakistan (2026)

Prices are per pack of 6 batteries from reputable brands:

Size 10 (Yellow) - For CIC/IIC Hearing Aids

The smallest battery, used in completely-in-canal and invisible hearing aids. Due to its small size, it has the shortest life span of 3-5 days per battery. A pack of 6 typically costs Rs. 350-600 depending on brand.

Size 312 (Brown) - For RIC and Small BTE

The most commonly used hearing aid battery. Fits most RIC hearing aids and compact BTEs. Each battery lasts 5-7 days. A pack of 6 costs approximately Rs. 350-600.

Size 13 (Orange) - For Standard BTE

Used in standard-sized BTE hearing aids. Provides 7-10 days of power per battery. A pack of 6 costs Rs. 350-550.

Size 675 (Blue) - For Power BTE

The largest hearing aid battery, used in Super Power and Ultra Power BTE devices for severe to profound hearing loss. Lasts 10-14 days. A pack of 6 costs Rs. 400-650.

Monthly Cost Estimate: For a single hearing aid using size 312 batteries at Rs. 500 per pack, you will spend approximately Rs. 250-350 per month on batteries. For two hearing aids, double that figure. Consider rechargeable hearing aids to eliminate battery costs entirely.

Tips to Maximize Battery Life

  • Wait after removing tab: After removing the protective tab from a new battery, wait 1-2 minutes before inserting it into your hearing aid. This allows the zinc-air cell to fully activate.
  • Open battery door at night: When not using your hearing aid, open the battery door to stop power drain and let moisture escape.
  • Store at room temperature: Do not refrigerate batteries. Store them in a cool, dry place away from metal objects.
  • Wash hands before handling: Grease and moisture from your hands can affect battery performance.
  • Use a drying kit: Moisture in the hearing aid makes the battery work harder. A dry hearing aid extends battery life.
  • Buy fresh batteries: Check expiry dates. Batteries lose charge over time even in the packaging.
