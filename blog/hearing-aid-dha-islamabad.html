Getting a Hearing Test

If your family member does not have a recent hearing test, they can visit the CMH (Combined Military Hospital) audiology department, any private ENT clinic near DHA, or come directly to our Blue Area center for a professional PTA hearing test. The test is complimentary with a hearing aid purchase.

Signs that a hearing test is needed include frequently asking people to repeat themselves, difficulty hearing doorbells or phone rings, turning the TV volume higher than others prefer, and withdrawing from group conversations. Early intervention makes a significant difference in outcomes.
