Diagnostic Services

Professional Hearing Tests

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of perfect hearing. Our certified audiologists use advanced diagnostic equipment.

PTA - Pure Tone Audiometry

The gold standard hearing test that identifies your exact hearing threshold levels. Essential for selecting the perfect hearing aid model and programming it precisely to your needs.

Tympanometry

Advanced middle ear assessment that evaluates eardrum mobility and middle ear pressure. Critical for diagnosing conductive hearing loss and ensuring accurate hearing aid fitting.
