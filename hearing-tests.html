PTA - Pure Tone Audiometry

Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA) is the best hearing test and the standard hearing loss test used to identify hearing threshold levels of an individual. This professional hearing assessment enables determination of the degree, type, and configuration of a hearing loss in Pakistan, providing a reliable basis for diagnosis and management by our qualified audiologists.


During this comprehensive hearing test, sounds of varying frequencies and volumes are played through headphones. The patient indicates when they can hear each sound, allowing the audiologist to map the exact pattern of hearing loss across different frequencies. This detailed hearing assessment helps our audiologists recommend the right hearing aid solution.


Who needs it: Anyone experiencing difficulty hearing conversations, TV, phone calls, or environmental sounds in their daily life. This hearing test for adults is typically the first test recommended by audiologists. If you're looking for a hearing test near you in Pakistan, PTA is essential for early detection of hearing problems.

Tympanometry

Tympanometry is an advanced hearing assessment and diagnostic examination used to test the condition of the middle ear and mobility of the eardrum (tympanic membrane) and the conduction bones by creating variations of air pressure in the ear canal. Our audiologists in Pakistan use this professional hearing test to provide accurate hearing loss diagnosis.


This comprehensive hearing test helps identify issues like fluid in the middle ear, eardrum perforations, Eustachian tube dysfunction, and otosclerosis. A small probe is placed in the ear canal, and air pressure changes are applied while measuring the eardrum's response. Tympanometry is one of the best hearing tests for detecting middle ear problems in adults and children.


Who needs it: Patients with suspected middle ear problems, recurrent ear infections, pressure issues, or conductive hearing loss. If you're looking for a hearing test near you in Pakistan and suspect hearing problems beyond simple hearing loss, tympanometry helps our audiologists identify the root cause and recommend appropriate treatment.

Why Get a Hearing Test?

A professional hearing test is essential for early detection of hearing loss. Early identification allows you to take action before hearing problems affect your quality of life, workplace performance, and social relationships. Our qualified audiologists in Pakistan recommend regular hearing assessments, especially if you're over 55 or exposed to loud environments.


Signs You May Need a Hearing Test: Do you struggle to hear conversations in noisy environments? Do people around you complain about TV volume? Do you miss phone calls or doorbells? These are common hearing loss signs that warrant a professional hearing assessment. A simple hearing test can confirm whether these symptoms indicate actual hearing problems.


Regular hearing assessments help our audiologists track changes in your hearing over time. This baseline data from your first hearing test enables us to detect gradual hearing loss before it becomes severe. Early intervention with the right hearing aid solution can prevent further deterioration and improve your listening experience immediately.
